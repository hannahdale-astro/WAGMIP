Who we are

A Space Where You Belong

WAGMIP is a welcoming community for women and gender minorities in physics — students, researchers, and anyone who loves the subject and wants to feel at home in it.

Physics can sometimes feel isolating, especially when you don't see yourself reflected in the room. We're here to change that — one Friday afternoon at a time.

Whether you want to talk about research, vent about problem sets, celebrate wins, or simply sit together with people who get it — WAGMIP is your place.
